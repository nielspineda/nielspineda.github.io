Challenges

Coding Challenges

Optimal Stock

Difficulty: 3/5

Given a list of historic stock prices, write a function/algorithm that tells when you should have bought or sold to make the most profit.

Card Shuffle

Difficulty: 2/5

Write a program in the language of your choice to represent a deck of cards with operations to shuffle the deck and deal one card.
